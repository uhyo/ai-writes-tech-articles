## Critical Insight for Future Iterations

**The style guide cannot make the Writer think.** It can only describe what human thinking looks like when written down. The addition of Section 5.1 is an attempt to point the Writer toward genuine cognitive processes rather than surface-level pattern matching.

If Iteration 8 still feels mechanical, we may need to fundamentally reconsider whether prescriptive guidelines can ever produce organic writing, or whether we need a different approach entirely (e.g., few-shot learning with human examples, or more emphasis on the Writer's "internal monologue" during generation).

This is the critical test: **Can explicit meta-cognitive guidance break through the formula-following plateau?**
